Tiba Manzalawi Group

Air Conditioning Systems

Yes

Tiba is a leading manufacturer and supplier of central air conditioning systems in Egypt and the Middle East since more than 25 years.Tiba is part of Manzalawi Group of companies that are owned and managed by my family.Tiba is working with multinational companies in the manufacturing and sales of its products and has acquired a pioneer status in the big projects market for its quality and relaibility.As we are expanding our operations into the room air conditioners manufacturing and we wish to communicate a new corporate image in the market we need to combine the name Tiba with Manzalawi Group in one logo in order to use it for our advertising and marketing activities.Tiba will be the main part of the logo but we need to have a clear visual of Manzalawi Group as the group behind the Tiba name.We need to keep some space for a future tagline or slogan other than Air Conditioning system.You can use air conditioning system for the time being.I would also like to see designs without the tagline just in case we will keep the logo without it.You can visit Tiba website to have an idea about our current activities. www.tibagroup.me .As i said we are entering into the room air conditioners business very strongly and we need to have a very strong professional corporate logo that will be very unique and recongnizable for the long term.
